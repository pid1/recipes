# Breakfast

## Pancakes

Ingredients:

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg

Instructions:

1. Mix all ingredients.
2. Cook on a hot griddle.

## Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

- 2 eggs
- 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:

1. Beat the eggs.
2. Cook in a pan with butter.

# Lunch

## Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

- 2 slices of bread
- 1 slice of cheese
- 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:

1. Butter the bread.
2. Place cheese between slices and grill.

# Dinner

## Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

- 200g spaghetti
- 100g ground beef
- 1 cup marinara sauce

Instructions:

1. Cook spaghetti.
2. Brown the beef and mix with marinara sauce.
3. Combine and serve.
